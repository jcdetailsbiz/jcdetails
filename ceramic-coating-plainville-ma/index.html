What Is Feynlab?

Not Your Average Ceramic Coating

Feynlab is a professional-only ceramic coating system — you won't find it at an auto parts store or on Amazon. It's sold exclusively through certified installers like JC Details LLC.

Feynlab offers a tiered coating system built for real-world performance. The Original and V3 are professional-grade single coat formulas — the V3 forms a lattice structure of silica, silicon-nitride, and silicon-carbide, delivering unmatched single-layer durability, industry-leading hydrophobics, UV protection, and heat resistance. The flagship Ultra V3 takes it further with bi-phasal chemistry, integrating a base coat and top coat into one bonded application to achieve a 113° water contact angle, superior self-cleaning, and a glass-like finish — performance levels conventional coatings simply can't reach.

Every tier bonds permanently to your paint and is available exclusively through certified installers like JC Details LLC.

FAQ

Ceramic Coating Questions Answered

Everything you need to know about Feynlab ceramic coating in Plainville, MA.

What is Feynlab ceramic coating? +
Feynlab is a professional-grade, certified-installer-only ceramic coating system. The V3 is a single coat formula that forms a lattice structure of silica, silicon-nitride, and silicon-carbide for unmatched durability, hydrophobics, and heat resistance. The flagship Ultra V3 uses bi-phasal chemistry to achieve a 113° water contact angle, superior self-cleaning, and a glass-like finish — performance conventional coatings can't reach. All tiers bond permanently to your paint and are sold exclusively through certified installers.
How long does a ceramic coating last? +
JC Details offers three Feynlab tiers: The Original Ceramic Coating (1-year protection, starting from $1,000), Feynlab V3 (3-year protection, starting from $1,500), and Feynlab Ultra V3 (5-year protection, starting from $2,000). Actual longevity depends on the package chosen and how well the vehicle is maintained after coating.
Does ceramic coating require paint correction first? +
Yes — and this is included in every JC Details ceramic coating package. Ceramic coating permanently bonds to your paint's current condition, so any swirl marks, scratches, or defects will be sealed in. We perform paint correction before every coating installation to ensure your paint looks its best.
Is JC Details mobile for ceramic coating? +
Yes. JC Details is 100% mobile. We bring all our professional equipment to your driveway or preferred location throughout Plainville, MA and surrounding towns including Wrentham, Franklin, Mansfield, North Attleborough, Foxborough, Attleboro, and parts of Rhode Island including Cumberland and Lincoln.
How do I maintain a ceramic-coated vehicle? +
Ceramic-coated vehicles are significantly easier to maintain. We recommend enrolling in our monthly wash plan to keep your coating performing at its best — bimonthly at most. Avoid automatic brush car washes, which can introduce micro-scratches, and never wax over the coating as it's unnecessary and can reduce performance.
Is ceramic coating worth it? +
For most vehicle owners, yes. A properly applied ceramic coating dramatically reduces the time and cost of maintenance, protects your paint from UV damage and environmental contaminants, and preserves resale value. A Feynlab coating applied over corrected paint is a significant upgrade from wax or sealants in terms of both durability and protection.
What's the difference between Feynlab and DIY ceramic coatings? +
Consumer-grade ceramic coatings sold in auto parts stores are formulated for easy application and are diluted compared to professional products. Feynlab is a certified-installer-only product — not available to the public — with a significantly higher concentration of active coating material. This translates to a harder, more durable coating that lasts longer and performs better than any off-the-shelf alternative.
