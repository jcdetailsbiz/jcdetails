Why a Maintenance Plan

Protect Your Investment, Effortlessly

A ceramic coating is only as good as the maintenance behind it. Neglected coatings lose hydrophobicity, accumulate bonded contaminants, and degrade years ahead of schedule.

A JC Details maintenance plan keeps your coating dialed in — regular washes, contamination removal, and coating inspections handled for you on a set schedule.

We come to your home or workplace every month so you never have to think about it.

Coating Longevity
